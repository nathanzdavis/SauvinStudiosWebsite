About Us

Sauvin Studios is a five-person indie team based in Spokane, Washington, driven by a shared passion for bold ideas and unforgettable experiences. We come from diverse creative backgrounds—art, music, storytelling, and code—united by the belief that great games are built on craft, originality, and heart. Our mission is to create emotionally resonant games that stand out across genres. Whether it’s a grimdark metroidvania, a satirical real-time strategy game, or a cozy life sim, each of our projects aims to push narrative, design, and atmosphere in meaningful ways. At Sauvin Studios, we’re building a long-term creative space where artistic risks are embraced, ideas are nurtured, and every game is made with intention. Our debut title, Bingus: REQUIEM, has a playable demo available now.
